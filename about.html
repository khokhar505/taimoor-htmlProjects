About Mushtaq Tanneries

Mushtaq Tannery founded in 1980 by Mr. Mushtaq Ahmad Kasur, Pakistan. The passion of tanning leather has passed from grandfather to grandsons today. Our leather adventure, has started in 1980 Since the beginning of the business, Mushtaq Tannery has been insisting on quality of leather, environment care and it's employees rights. Mushtaq Tannery with its wide range of products and innovations, produce finished sheepskin leather, lambskin leather and bovine leather with veg tan leather or chrome tanned leather. Mushtaq Tannery has strong investments in leather tanning technology, continues today with the modern facilities in the heart of the leather Kasur, Pakistan.Mushtaq Tannery manufacturing leather for many countries of the world in our modern facilities under the management of our friendly management we took a more innovative and modern structure and now Mushtaq Tannery, which has a significant place in the Pakistan and world leather market. Mushtaq Tannery provides services in sectors such as primarily clothing, handbags and shoes. High quality leather is produced by continuously developing the articulates for fashion by its strong R&D department, innovative structure and by the machinery park of the latest technology in Mushtaq Tannery, with having a production capacity for 6.000 skins (approximately 40.000 Square feet) of tanned leather hides daily. The most demanded leather articles of Mushtaq Tannery by it's customers are; Nappa Leather, Aniline Leather, Crust Leather, Shruken Leather , Bovine Leather and Full Grain Leather. Mushtaq Tannery always concerned in projects designed to enforce tanning methods and techniques aimed at optimizing efficient, organized and innovative leather tanning able to ensure an economic growing with also protect the environment, humans health and the living beings in the past, present and in the future.
